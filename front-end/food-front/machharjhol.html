Machhar Jhol

Machhar Jhol is a flavorful Bengali fish curry made with fresh fish and a blend of spices, served with rice for a hearty meal.

Time: 45 minutes

Protein:

  • Fish (500g) provides about 100g of protein.
  • Vegetables (optional) add additional protein.

Recipe Details

Ingredients

Steps to Make Machhar Jhol:

Listen to the instructions here:

  1. Marinate 500g of fish with salt and turmeric. Set aside for about 15-20 minutes.
  2. In a large pan or kadhai, heat the mustard oil until it starts to smoke. Reduce the heat to medium.
  3. Carefully add the marinated fish pieces to the hot oil. Fry until they are golden brown on both sides. Remove and set aside.
  4. In the same oil, add the bay leaves and whole garam masala. Saut√© for a minute.
  5. Add the sliced onions and cook until they turn golden brown.
  6. Stir in the ginger and garlic paste. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  7. Add the chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and slit green chilies. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the oil starts to separate.
  8. Pour in enough water to make a gravy (about 1-2 cups, depending on your preference). Bring it to a boil.
  9. Gently add the fried fish pieces to the boiling gravy. If using, add the cubed potatoes at this stage. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, allowing the fish to absorb the flavors.
  10. Taste and adjust salt as needed. If using, add Panch Phoron for extra flavor.
  11. Once done, garnish with chopped coriander leaves. Serve hot with steamed rice.
