Lassi

Lassi is a traditional Indian yogurt-based drink that is both refreshing and nutritious. It can be enjoyed sweet or savory, making it a versatile beverage.

Time: 10 minutes

Protein:

  • Yogurt (1 cup) provides around 8g of protein.
  • Milk (if used) adds another 8g of protein per cup.
  • Optional nuts (like almonds) can contribute additional protein.

Recipe Details

Ingredients

Steps to Make Lassi:

Listen to the instructions here:

  1. In a blender, combine 1 cup of yogurt, 1/2 cup of water, and sugar or salt to taste.
  2. Add a pinch of cardamom powder for flavor, if desired.
  3. Blend until smooth and frothy.
  4. Pour into glasses and garnish with mint leaves or a sprinkle of cardamom.
  5. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Variations:

  • Fruity Lassi: Add fruits like mango, strawberries, or bananas to the blender for a fruity twist.
  • Savory Lassi: Skip the sugar and add spices like cumin or black salt for a savory version.
