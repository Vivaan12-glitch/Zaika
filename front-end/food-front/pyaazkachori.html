Pyaaz Kachori

Pyaaz Kachori is a delicious Indian snack made with spiced onion filling encased in a crispy outer layer, deep-fried until golden brown.

Time: 1 hour

Protein:

  • Whole wheat flour (1 cup) provides about 10g of protein.
  • Chickpea flour (if used) adds approximately 22g of protein per cup.

Ingredients

Steps to Make Pyaaz Kachori:

Listen to the instructions here:

  1. Soak the Urad Dal and Idli Rice (if using) for at least 4 hours or overnight. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, mix 2 cups of whole wheat flour with salt and water to form a smooth dough. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. In a pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil. Add 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds, and once they splutter, add 2 cups of finely chopped onions. Saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Stir in the green chilies and 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste. Cook for another minute, then add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well and cook until the mixture is dry. Remove from heat and add chopped coriander leaves. Let it cool.
  5. Divide the dough into small balls. Take one ball and flatten it slightly, then roll it out into a small circle. Place a tablespoon of the filling in the center, gather the edges to seal, and gently roll it out again into a thick disc.
  6. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the kachoris until golden brown, then drain on paper towels.
  7. Serve hot with chutney (like tamarind or mint chutney) and yogurt, if desired.
