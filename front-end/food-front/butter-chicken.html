Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a rich and creamy North Indian dish made with marinated chicken simmered in a fragrant tomato and cream-based sauce.

Time: 1 hour

Protein:

  • Chicken (500g) provides around 110g of protein.
  • Yogurt (1 cup) adds 10g of protein.
  • Cream (1/2 cup) adds 4g of protein.

Ingredients

Steps to Make Butter Chicken:

Listen to the instructions here:

  1. In a bowl, combine the yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and mix well to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (or overnight for best results).
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat and add a little oil. Add the marinated chicken pieces (discard excess marinade) and cook until fully cooked and slightly charred. Remove from heat and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add butter and a tablespoon of oil. Once melted, add the chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  5. Add the slit green chilies and ginger-garlic paste, and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  6. Pour in the tomato puree and cook for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally until the oil separates from the sauce.
  7. Add red chili powder, garam masala, and cumin powder to the sauce. Stir well to combine.
  8. Add the cooked chicken pieces to the sauce and mix thoroughly.
  9. Lower the heat and stir in the cream and kasuri methi. Cook for an additional 5-10 minutes until everything is heated through and the flavors meld.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
